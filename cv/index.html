through the use of static analysis, formal verification, functional programming and advanced type systems.

My academic background is a Ph.D. in Computer Science on the topic of automated theorem proving.

Over the last 15 years I have worked as a software developer within Cloud, Web, Telecom and Automotive.

I have worked with several different tech stacks, my preferred day-to-day ones are C, Python, Scala and Haskell.
